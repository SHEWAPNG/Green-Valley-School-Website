About Us

The Green Valley School Association is dedicated to enhancing the educational experience of students through community engagement, fundraising, and volunteer activities. Founded in 1985, we have been supporting our school community for over 35 years.

Our mission is to foster a collaborative environment between parents, teachers, and administrators to ensure every student receives the best possible education and opportunities.
