About Us

Our History

The Green Valley School Association was established in 1985 by a group of dedicated parents and teachers who recognized the need for a collaborative approach to education. Over the past 35+ years, we have grown from a small committee to a thriving organization with hundreds of members.

Throughout our history, we have funded numerous projects, including playground renovations, technology upgrades, library expansions, and scholarship programs. Our annual fundraising events have become beloved community traditions that bring together families, educators, and local businesses.

Our Mission

At Green Valley School Association, our mission is to enhance the educational experience of every student by:

  • Fostering communication between parents, teachers, and administrators
  • Raising funds for educational materials and experiences not covered by the school budget
  • Organizing volunteer opportunities that support school activities
  • Advocating for policies and programs that benefit our students
  • Building a strong, inclusive community that celebrates diversity
